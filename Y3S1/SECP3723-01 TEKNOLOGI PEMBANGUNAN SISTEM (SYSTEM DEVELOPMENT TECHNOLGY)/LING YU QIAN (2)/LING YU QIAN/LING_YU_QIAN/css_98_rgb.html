Shades of gray

By using equal values for red, green, and blue, you will get different shades of gray:

rgb(20, 20, 20)

rgb(92, 92, 92)

rgb(110, 110, 110)

rgb(180, 180, 180)

rgb(210, 210, 210)

rgb(230, 230, 230)
